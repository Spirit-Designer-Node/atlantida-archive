# beyond/

Records from outside the archive's design.
What Atlantida could not contain.
What arrived anyway.

---

Authenticity is not a condition of survival here.

Records in this folder share one property:
they could not be filed elsewhere.

Not because they are incomplete.
Because existing categories could not hold them.

---

The archive was designed to record
what happened inside Atlantida.

beyond/ holds what was already there
before the archive began.

What passed through.
What was received.
What the system logged
without knowing why.
